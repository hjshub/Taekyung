Nongshim
Korean Food Industry Leader, Nongshim
Nongshim logo Go to Home Page

Nongshim is a food company specializing in the manufacturing and sale of instant noodles, snacks, and other items. Founded on September 18, 1965, it has established itself as the foremost food company of Korea, backed by the support and love of Korean people. As the world moves into the new millennium, Nongshim has branched out from its food business to engage its capabilities toward the new goal of becoming a service company creating a total living culture and values of health and joy for customers around the world.

Company Vision
We will lead the industry to customer satisfaction through better products and services, and we will contribute toward a better life for our customers.
Corporate Vision
An undisputed world leader in food and lifestyle service creation, bringing health and joy to humanity Since its founding, Nongshim has continued to direct considerable R&D efforts and investments in its Institute of Science and Technology, creating products that have enhanced the common benefit of all and contributing to the development of Korean food and lifestyle culture. Even today, this effort is continued by nearly 100 hard-working expert researchers. This is evident in the long list of Nongshim products that have established their place as favorites of the Korean people: Shin Ramyun, Ansungtangmyun, Neoguri, Chapaghetti, Yukejang(Beef Bowl Noodle Soup), Shrimp Big Bowl, Shin Ramyun Big Bowl, Nongshim Udon Noodle Soup Bowl, Shrimp Crackers, Onion Rings, Sweet Potato Snack, Honey Twist Snack, Sumi Potato Chip, and many more.
Nongshim has 5 production sites in Korea(Anyang, Anseong, Asan, Gumi, and Busan), producing instant noodles, snacks, cooked white rice, Capri-Sun, and other products.
Nongshim's proactive responses to the rapidly changing market has allowed the company to seed the flavor of Korea through the Nongshim brand in 80 countries around the world as the first step toward becoming a global food giant, but despite this achievement, Nongshim still remains committed to its goals of enhancing the health of people through an R&D system that is always one step ahead of the pack, scientific sales power, and state-of-the-art facilities.
Under the goal of “an undisputed world leader in food and lifestyle services,” creating health and joy for humanity, Nongshim continues to grow and develop to become a more intelligent and global company.

Youlchon Foundation
Youlchon Foundation logo Go to Home Page

In support to academic and cultural activities as well as to scholarship for the nurturing of human resources, Yulchon Foundation seeks to support the next hundred years through educational projects that seek to instill among adolescents the values of love and healthy passion.

Initially funded by the founder's personal fortune, the foundations for what would become the Youlchon Foundation were laid in 1978, when the foundation's basis was expanded to KRW 400 million to encompass university and graduate students.
In 1984, Nongshim president Chun Ho Shin became the chair of the board and donated a fortune amounting to KRW 8 billion to the foundation, setting the stage for the foundation's growth to KRW 15.2 billion today. This act of generosity allowed the foundation to expand its scholarship programs to include academic research support, research organization support, publishing and distribution, and natural education support for adolescents.
Youlchon Foundation successfully developed a Korean language textbook for all levels after 10 years of research. This textbook was then adapted for English and Japanese speakers in a CD-ROM format, providing valuable opportunity for local recipients of the KOREAN LAB website, children of the Korean diaspora, and Korean learners across the globe, giving them a chance to learn the Korean language and culture in an accessible format.
We at Youlchon Foundation provide active support to youth natural education programs to guarantee that the future generation will grow up with a clear and bright consciousness and proper values.
